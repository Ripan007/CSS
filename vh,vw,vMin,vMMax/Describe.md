# vh,vw,vMin,vMax:

- vh : viewPort height
- vw : viewPort width
- causing issue in case of smaller device , content get overflow when we resize the screen (overflow content)

## vh,vw

- not depend upon the container
- depend upon the viewPort

## vMin

- look at the smaller of the 2 unit

## vMax

- look at the bigger unit

## %

- depend upon the parent container
